Welcome to Doremi, an open-source Discord bot designed to help you manage and enhance your server with ease.

Our mission is to provide easy-to-use, customizable tools for all your server management needs.

Here are some of the key features of doremi:

  • Comprehensive moderation tools
  • Customizable commands and responses
  • Fun features to engage your community

Doremi is developed and maintained by teaditional. I am always looking for feedback and contributions from the community. If you have any suggestions or would like to contribute to the project, please join our support server.
