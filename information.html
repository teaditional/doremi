Information

Welcome to lilly, an open-source Discord bot designed to help you manage and enhance your server with ease. Whether you need moderation tools, fun commands, or custom features, lilly has you covered.

Our mission is to provide easy-to-use, customizable tools for all your server management needs.

Here are some of the key features of lilly:

  • Comprehensive moderation tools
  • Customizable commands and responses
  • Fun features to engage your community

lilly is developed and maintained by teaditional. I am always looking for feedback and contributions from the community. If you have any suggestions or would like to contribute to the project, please join our support server.
